Circle of Fifths

I drew out a circle of fifths in my house, just pen and paper. The circle of fifths is a very useful tool for finding out key signatures since we use the phrase: Father Charles goes down and ends battle for the sharps, and for the flats it’s the other way round, battle end and down goes Charles’s father. We use this to figure out the key signature.
